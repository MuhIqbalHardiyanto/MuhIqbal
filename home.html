WELCOME TO FAMS

Portrait of a family consisting of father, mother, child, son-in-law, and grandchild.
The family is the smallest unit of society which consists of the head of the family and
several people who are collected and live somewhere under a roof in a state of mutual
dependence. [1] According to Salvicion and Celis (1998) in the family there are two or more than two
individuals who are joined because of blood relations, marital relationships or
appointments, in their lives in one household, interacting with each other and in their
respective roles and creating and maintaining a culture. [2]
Based on Law 52 of 2009 concerning the Development of Population and Family Development,
Chapter I Article 1 paragraph 6 understanding The family is the smallest unit in the community consisting of husband and wife; or
husband, wife and children; or father and child (widower), or mother and child (widow).
